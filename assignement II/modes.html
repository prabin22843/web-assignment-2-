Game Modes

  • Solo: Play against 99 players in the lobby and try to survive alone.
  • Duo: Team up with one friend and compete with other pairs of players.
  • Squad: Make a team of 4 players and play against other squads.

Arctic Mode
  • Classic Mode: The standard battle royale mode where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. It involves survival, looting, and strategic gameplay on large maps like Erangel, Miramar, and Sanhok.
  • Arcade Mode: A fast-paced mode with smaller maps and shorter matches, featuring unique game types like Quick Match, War Mode, or Sniper Training.
  • Arctic Mode: A survival-based mode set in snowy conditions where players must battle the cold, gather resources, and stay warm while fending off enemies.

Popular PUBG Maps

  • Erangel: A classic map preferred by millions of gamers due to its simplicity and exciting features.
  • Miramar: A desert-themed map widely known for long-range battles.
  • Sanhok: A smaller jungle map for quick action.
  • Vikendi: A snow-covered map with beautiful views and quick action.
